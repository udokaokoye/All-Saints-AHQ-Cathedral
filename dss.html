DAILY SPIRITUAL STRENGTH (DSS)


Thursday, April 30

TEXT - Isaiah 30:21

(Your own ears will hear him. Right behind you a voice will say, "This is the way you should go," whether to the right or to the left.) In as much as the law guides our daily life and makes sure we don't fall out of the path of God, you never go wrong when the spirit of God is leading and guiding your path. Most times unaware of the happenings in the spirit realm but you somehow find yourself taking directions and decision as though someone is speaking to you, this is because the holy spirit is always there “In the same way the Spirit also helps our weakness; for we do not know how to pray as we should, but the Spirit Himself intercedes for us with groanings too deep for words; and He who searches the hearts knows what the mind of the Spirit is, because He intercedes for the saints according to the will of God Romans 8:26-27” Often at times you keep hearing a tiny voice inside of you trying to either give guidance, even when the situation might not be suited to our needs but believers should never forget that when you are led by the Spirit of God the end result is always rewarding. (Matthew 4) We see how the Holy Spirit tailors gifts that suit individual potentials; believers should know that the Holy Spirit understands individual variations and how best to lead them. So, rather than trying to figure how to navigate your life through the law, why not step back and let the Holy Spirit lead. David understood it better when he said in Psalms 23 '…He leadeth me beside the still waters…' Who is leading you? Memorise: 2 Corinthians 3:4- “And we have such trust through Christ towards God.”

Insight: THE SPIRIT STILL SPEAKS TO MEN.
